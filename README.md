# ashes
### by fênix team
****
"every legend begins in the ashes of something that refused to disappear"
****
Ashes is a 2D boss rush style game where each boss represents other teams in the Uniars championship:
##### Fênix Red Team
Represented by a Phoenix, entity from the Greek mythology.
##### Charía Blue Team
Represented by a Blue Jaguar, entity from the Brazilian "Tupi Guarani" tribe.
##### Fenrir Black Team
Represented by a Black Wolf, entity from the Norse mythology.
##### Grifo Purple Team
Represented by a Purple Griffin, entity from the Greek mythology.
##### Wyvern Green Team
Represented by a Green Drangon.
##### Apolo Yellow Team
Represented by Apollo, entity from the Greek mythology.
##### Pegasus White Team
Represented by a White Winged Horse, entity from the Greek mythology.
##### Scorpion Orange Team
Represented by a Orange Scorpion.
